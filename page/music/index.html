Album Recommendations

Hymn The Narcissist Cookbook

2019

Incredible folk punk album from The Narcissist Cookbook. "A story about grief and perfectionism", executed with a mixture of song and spoken word tracks. "Meditative and poetic", and an incredible introduction to The Narcissist Cookbook. My personal favourite track is Unwelcome Guests, a poem about being haunted by the memories of your past. It never fails to being me to tears.

Pornography The Cure

1982

This album was both my introduction to The Cure and goth music, both of which I have come to love. This remains my favouirte album from The Cure, it's such an iconic sound that I've really connected to over dozens of listens this year. My favourite song off the album is probably the titular song, Pornography, but it's hard to pick any stand out between these 8 tracks.
